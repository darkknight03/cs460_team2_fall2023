Mastercard payment cards in Russia

Mastercard has decided to restrict its activities in Russia. This also affects the payment cards of Viseca Card Services SA. Until further notice, our cards can therefore neither be used in Russia (shops as well as ATM) nor in Russian online shops.

Visa payment cards in Russia

Visa has decided to restrict its activities in Russia. This also affects the payment cards of Viseca Card Services SA. Until further notice, our cards can therefore neither be used in Russia (shops as well as ATM) nor in Russian online shops.
