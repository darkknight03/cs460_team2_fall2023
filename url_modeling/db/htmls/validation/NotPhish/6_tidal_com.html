FAQ

Can I try TIDAL free of charge?

Yes! You can try TIDAL Free, which gives you access to our deep music catalogue with limited ad interruptions. Or you can sign up for a free trial of TIDAL HiFi or HiFi Plus for an ad-free, fully interactive listening experience.

Can I import my playlists from other streaming services I use?

Yes, we want your transition to TIDAL to be as smooth as possible. You can import playlists here.

What subscription options does TIDAL offer?

We have a range of subscription options across our HiFi and HiFi Plus tiers. In addition to our Individual payment plan, we offer a low-cost Family plan (you plus 5 users), and discounted Student, Military, and First Responder plans.
