Create Unique Personalized Gifts

Treat your loved ones to something special with personalized gifts from Shutterfly. By uploading their favorite photos, adding text, and customizing the colors, you can create one-of-a-kind photo gifts your recipient will love. From coffee mugs and puzzles to phone cases, water bottles, and more — the opportunities are endless when you create personalized gifts with Shutterfly. From gifts for mom and dad, to kids gifts, and birthday gifts — they'll appreciate the thought and effort put into making a personalized gift just for them. 

Send Cards and Invitations

Celebrate every special occasion with custom greeting cards and invitations. Whether you're sending birthday cards, Christmas cards, wedding invitations, or simply a photo card that lets your recipient know you're thinking of them — there's a Shutterfly card for every occasion. Send life's biggest updates with a greeting card that will stand out among the rest because it's created by you. Whether you're making cards for your graduation day or your upcoming baby shower, use Shutterfly's invitation maker to create the perfect invites of your dreams. Design each card with photos, text, trim styles, and high-quality paper types to make each one completely your style and perfect for your occasion. For more cards and stationery, you can create business cards, monthly planners, notebooks, and more.

Make Photo Books for Cherished Memories

Your most memorable moments belong in a photo book. Shutterfly has everything you need to create amazing custom photo books you’ll love. Whether you're capturing funny moments with loved ones or putting together wedding photo albums from your big day, you can make the best photo books with Shutterfly. Photo books are not only perfect for highlighting all of your favorite pictures, but they also make the perfect gift or keepsake for yourself or a loved one. With one-of-a-kind cover styles, page layouts, embellishments, there are no limits to what you can do when you make a photo book with Shutterfly.

Print Photos to Save Every Moment

Remember all of your favorite memories with photo prints from Shutterfly. Instead of letting your most-loved photos live only in your phone's camera roll or a hard drive, print them so you can save them forever as photo prints or unique wall art decor. With Shutterfly, you can hang your prints, save them in a photo album, or display them in a picture frame as beautiful pieces of home decor. Photos add so much warmth and personality to any room, and no matter what you're printing - — art prints, photo prints, posters, and more — you can bring your most cherished images to life with Shutterfly. 

Design Home Decor and Wall Art

Personalized home decor is the best way to display the images that mean the most to you. Turn your captured memories of your wedding day, your children's milestones, and favorite vacations into custom home decor items perfect for your living space. Explore our selection of wall art such as canvas prints and photo tiles, or cozy items like photo pillows and fleece blankets. Shutterfly has the perfect home decor elements to compliment your space's style — from elegant and modern pieces to farmhouse wall decor. Add a unique touch to your home with one-of-a-kind drinkware, framed prints, personalized cookbooks, and other home goods that will double as unique keepsakes. No matter what room you're looking to design home decor for, Shutterfly has the perfect product to suit you and your family's style.
