        Dear passengers, 

        In accordance with the decision of the Ministry of Interior of the Republic of Türkiye, as of March 3, 2022, passengers are no longer required to submit a negative PCR test result for domestic flights and the HES Code is not required to be submitted for any of our flights.

        Please note that it is still mandatory to wear a mask during the flight.

        We recommend that you review our important announcements page for developments related to domestic and international travel due to COVID-19, including flights suspended between countries and similar information. Please see our travel requirements pages for information regarding the rules applicable to arrival in Türkiye and country-specific travel restrictions.
