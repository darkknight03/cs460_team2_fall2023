BONJOUR ! JE SUIS CELTINA

In 2012, I set up S.L.Immersion (Sjour Linguistique en Immersion). Before that, I have been teaching French for foreigners in schools in Lyon and Switzerland but I wanted to offer more authentic, intensive experiences that are different to other learning centres.

I started to welcome students at my house to live and study the real French today with me. As my French courses in Provence became known and very popular due to the success with students, I expanded the offers in France with the help of local teachers, passionate about their region and the French language. Hence, I have personally selected each teacher with high certification and experience in teaching. Our programs provide high quality stay in total immersion with private and adaptable courses: a combination of Learning, Culture, Exchange with the local, and Authenticity.

Im glad to organise for you the perfect programme of authentic immersion language homestay or personalised online one to one sessions.

Celtina, your personal counsellor.
