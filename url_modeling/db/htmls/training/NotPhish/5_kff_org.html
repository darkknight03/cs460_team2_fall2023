The Burden of Medical Debt in the U.S.

Our analysis finds 9% of adults – or roughly 23 million people – owe medical debt, including 11 million who owe more than $2,000. Americans likely owe hundreds of billions of dollars in total medical debt, which is significantly higher other commonly cited estimates. The report also examines variations in medical debt by age, race and ethnicity, health status, and for people with a disability.
