Security, Privacy & Trust

At least 90% of our customers digital currencies are stored offline in geographically redundant secure locations. Wherever practicable, we also use multi-sig technology to protect digital assets that must be stored online.

SECURITY

We employ multiple strategies to protect our customer's assets and information, including data encryption, Transport Layer Security, periodic security audits, organisation security and enhanced security features for users.

PRIVACY

Your personal and financial information is only stored and processed in cloud services that meet our strict infrastructure security requirements. Its only collected and shared in accordance with our privacy policy.
