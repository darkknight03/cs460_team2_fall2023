Description

This app displays and monitors the latest prices of the top 100 most valuable cryptocurrencies and also include customizable today screen widget (1500+ cryptocurrencies)


This app displays and monitors latest prices of top 100 most valuable cryptocurrencies.

App features:

- Real-time price monitoring

- Customisable today screen widget (over 1500 cryptocurrencies to choose from)

- detailed graphs over 24 hour, week, month, 6 month, year

- drag finger across graph to view price at precise date

- conversion of cryptocurrencies to different currencies
