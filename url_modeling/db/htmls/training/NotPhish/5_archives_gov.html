Coronavirus Information

The National Archives and Records Administration is committed to protecting the health and safety of visitors, customers, and employees during the COVID-19 (coronavirus) pandemic. The National Archives Museum in Washington, DC, is open, and Presidential Library museums are open with limited capacity.

As local public health metrics allow, National Archives research rooms will open for research on a limited basis. We are making every effort to continue providing essential services whenever possible, using online and remote capabilities. Our ability to serve customers in a timely manner may be hampered by the current crisis, and we ask for your understanding and patience. Check our coronavirus information page for updates.
