TO OUR ADMITTED BRUINS, THEIR FAMILIES AND FRIENDS

Thank you for making us better. UCLA received more applications this year than any other university in the country. Nearly 150,000 extraordinary high school seniors from around the world wanted to study here. Despite the incredible caliber of these applicants, we could only accept fewer than 1 in 9. We are so thrilled with our admitted Bruins, who we know will add to the vibrancy, diversity and innovative thinking that make UCLA so special. We thank the teachers and mentors who helped guide them, and the families who shaped and supported them. Most of all, we thank the exceptional students for their hard work, determination and commitment, especially during these challenging times. We believe in your potential and will do everything in our power to help you realize it. So, for all you did to get to this moment, your Bruin family would just like to say: Thank you. We are proud of you. Congratulations to our newest Bruins.

Sincerely,
