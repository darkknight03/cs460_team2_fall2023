Diversity, equity and inclusion

We seek to unleash the potential of all people. Your background, situation, gender or physical ability should not dictate your access to higher education. From inspiring students to take up tertiary study, to alternative entry pathways and admission requirements, well provide you the support to succeed throughout your degree.
