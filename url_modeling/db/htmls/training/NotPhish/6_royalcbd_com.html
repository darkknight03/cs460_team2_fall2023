The Royal Promise

At Royal CBD, our main priority is the quality of our products. We're tired of seeing low-quality CBD oil being passed off as effective because we know it isn't. We refuse to settle into the mediocrity of the CBD industry at large and spend the time and effort needed to make products that are actually worth buying.

How We Do It

Making premium hemp requires meticulous attention to detail. We focus on sourcing nothing but the best hemp we can find, hire skilled staff to run the extractions, and test everything.

Farming

The key to making high-grade hemp extracts is to start at the source. We secure top-notch hemp plants from local California farms. The strain we select was bred specifically to have a rich terpene and cannabinoid profile. Our farmers use sustainable practices and apply organic farming methods to preserve both the quality of the hemp and the planet.

Extraction

All of our products are made using a whole-plant solventless CO2 extraction. This means there are no harsh chemicals and minimal breakdown of those precious cannabinoids or terpenes during the extraction process.

Manufacturing

Not only do we adhere to the national Current Good Manufacturing Practices (CGMPs), we aim to go above and beyond. This means testing each batch individually, using state-of-the-art extraction technology, and hiring only the best technicians to get the job done right each and every time. Nothing leaves our factory without a stamp of approval by a qualified third-party laboratory.
