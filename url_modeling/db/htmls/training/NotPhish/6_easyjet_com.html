      All flight prices:

       *FREEDOM TO CHANGE:

      To give you extra flexibility this year, we are currently waiving our flight change fee for changes made up to 2 hours before departure. Applies to changes made up until the 31st of March 2022, to any flights currently on sale in our schedule and to any destination. Standard terms and conditions apply to all changes. If the new fare is higher, you will be required to pay the fare difference. No credit is given if the new fare is lower. All changes must be made at least 2 hours before departure. Changes can be made on Manage Bookings or on our Mobile App. Change fees may apply for changes made after the current fee waiver has ended. 

      **The UK Government have announced that from 18th March 2022, you won’t need to fill out a Passenger Locator Form or take any tests to fly to the UK. For more information, please visit: https://www.easyjet.com/en/help/preparing-to-fly/latest-travel-information. Please check the entry requirements of the countries you’re heading to, as restrictions may still apply.

      Manage your cookies
      We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. Use the toggles to allow them all or manage them individually.
      We need these cookies to allow our site to work.
      These help us enhance your online experience. They’re not essential, but some bits of functionality might not work, for example, our chat bot or our feedback buttons. Turning this off also stops us from recommending deals specific to you when you’re on our site.
      When you allow easyJet marketing cookies, it lets us to use your data to make recommendations to you when you use other websites.  These cookies allow our advertising partners to collect information and customise ads based on what you look at. Without these cookies, we won’t be able to show you personalised ads based on your interests, instead they’ll be more generic.
