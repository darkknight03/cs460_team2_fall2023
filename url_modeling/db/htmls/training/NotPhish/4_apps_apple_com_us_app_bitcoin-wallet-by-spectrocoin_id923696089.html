Description

SpectroCoin blockchain wallet is designed to be functional, secure and easy to navigate on your iPhone or iPad. Get a free Ethereum, NEM, Dash, USDT, BNK or Bitcoin wallet and deposit cryptocurrency to your account, receive and send, sell and buy bitcoins (BTC) and other altcoins, exchange them to a variety of fiat currencies. With a few swipes you can instantly transact with anyone in the world, manage your account balance and get insightful news.

Securely store your funds in a SpectroCoin blockchain wallet because managing your financial operations on the go just got easier and faster!
