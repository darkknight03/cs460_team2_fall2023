Jamie Dornan & Wife Amelia Warner Keep Close While Arriving at Oscar Wilde Awards 2022

Jamie Dornan and Amelia Warner are stepping out for the night!

The 39-year-old Belfast actor and his musician wife, also 39, posed together on the green carpet as they arrived at the 2022 Oscar Wilde Awards on Thursday night (March 24) at The Ebell Club in Los Angeles.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Jamie Dornan

For the event, Jamie looked handsome in a black suit while Amelia wore a yellow, black, and gray knit vest paired with a long, black and gray plaid skirt.

Hosted by the US-Ireland Alliance, the awards event honors Irish contributions to Hollywood in film, television and music.

Jamie was honored during the awards show for his role in Belfast.

“Jamie has spoken about how much this role meant to him — both in terms of how lucky it is to be in a film about your hometown and about how this role allows him to pay homage to his father, who died before being able to see this film,” Trina Vargo, founder and president of the US-Ireland Alliance said in a statement via THR. “Belfast is very personal to Jamie, and that shines through in his moving performance.”

If you missed it, Jamie recently shared a very rare photo of his and Amelia‘s three daughters!
