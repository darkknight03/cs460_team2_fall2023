Description

This is the official CoinCap.io mobile app. Enjoy real time cryptocurrency market data, with features available only in the app:

kjneuner , 01/06/2018

The best.

This app is awesome. Not only does it support tons of different cryptocurrencies and update in real time, the altfolio feature is hands-down one of the most useful things for someone who wants to know the value of ALL of their cryptocurrencies at once. It’s easy to add and subtract from your holdings, or even have a couple different altfolios if you want to separate them out for whatever reason. The alert feature is also VERY robust and can even integrate with your altfolios. A couple of critiques: when you first reopen the app, it’s sometimes frozen for a few seconds while it catches up, the alerts don’t always get sent out like they should, and the prices can sometimes fluctuate pretty aggressively pretty quickly (~+/-1-2%). I’d like to know where the app gets its data. It usually seems to be right around where coinmarketcap is, so it seems to be accurate, but the fluctuations just invite some curiosity. I would also really like to see a 1hr graph on the price charts, and this app would make for an *awesome* Apple Watch app, should the devs decide to be ambitious :D As it stands now, though, the app is just about perfect, and I would recommend it to any holder of crypto who wants to know the value of their holdings in a simple, visually appealing, easy to use way.

If you’re into crypto, this app will become your new bestfriend

I’ve recently hopped on the crypto bandwagon. It’s been about two months now, and overall, it’s been a hectic ride. There’s a unique emotional thrill that comes with watching your coin assets fluctuate throughout the weeks. A feeling I’ve been lucky to experience a handful amount of times.


This app, CoinCap, adds the cherry on top.


No other app on the market offers such a clean and simple UI to describe the chaotic nature of cryptocurrencies. CoinCap allows the user to quickly add/remove/edit (from a wide range of various coins/tokens!) your exchanged assets into a personal “altfolio.” This live-fed mechanism allows you to keep track of your asset values second-to-second.


Lastly, you’ll have the ability to set alarms of various kinds for any coin you wish. This allows the frequent traders to be notified when to buy/sell/exchange to achieve your highest profit.


This app was exactly what I was looking for, and I’m very glad I found it.
