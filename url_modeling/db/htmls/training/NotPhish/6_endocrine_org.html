Meetings & Events

ENDO 2022 Registration Is Open

February 28, 2022

Register now for ENDO 2022! This year registrants have the choice to attend either in person or virtually, so you may participate as you prefer. Our program features more than 100 in-person and virtual sessions and an additional 50+ in-person only sessions. Be sure to secure your spot for the top annual meeting in endocrinology.

Meetings & Events

Transition Into a New Career Successfully

March 23, 2022

Widen your understanding of new career options in industry and academia with our webinar, Transitioning Careers: Industry and Academia, on Monday, March 28 at 1:00 PM EDT. Learn how to apply your knowledge and skills across fields firsthand from those that have already made successful career changes. Register today! All career stages are encouraged to participate.

Awards

Showcase Your Research's Artistry

March 10, 2022

Share the beauty of your microscopy images with your peers through our Endocrine Images competition. The top images selected by our member volunteers will be displayed at ENDO 2022 and may be featured on the Society’s website, magazine, and social media channels. Submissions will be accepted through March 25, 2022.

Membership

Message of Support for Ukrainian People

March 10, 2022

The news from Ukraine is distressing to us all. As a global organization committed to human health worldwide and with members in Ukraine, we want to support the Ukrainian people. We are asking our members who also feel inclined to help to consider donating to the International Committee of the Red Cross. Click above to learn more and donate.

Advocacy

Advocacy in Action

March 17, 2022

We continue to fight anti-transgender affirming care bills in Texas and other states.  We encourage our members to use the Society's  transgender advocacy  resources, and stand firm by our Texas members who continue to provide gender affirming care to patients based on medical evidence, not politics.

Endocrine Society News

Virtual Science Writers Conference to cover COVID-19 and the future of obesity, diabetes care

March 16, 2022

Experts will discuss how COVID-19 affects obesity and diabetes treatment during a virtual Science Writers Conference on March 29.

People with diabetes who eat less processed food at night may live longer

March 15, 2022

The time of day that people with diabetes eat certain foods may be just as important to their well-being as portion size and calories, according to a new study published in the Endocrine Society’s Journal of Clinical Endocrinology and Metabolism.

Endocrine Society streamlines name change policy for journal authors

March 03, 2022

The Endocrine Society has introduced a policy to make it simpler for authors of articles published in its peer-reviewed journals to update their names following a name change.

Featured Journal Articles

Post-Dexamethasone Free Cortisol

March 22, 2022

This study aims to determine the normal ranges of free cortisol during dexamethasone suppression tests (DST) in participants without adrenal disorders and to identify the population of patients where post-DST free cortisol measurements add value to the diagnostic workup.

INHBB Gene Variant Alters Testis Germ Cell Content

March 22, 2022

In this study, we aim to test the causality of the p.Met360Thr variant in INHBB and testis function.

Whole-Exome Sequencing in Hyperinsulinemia

March 22, 2022

In this article, we report a case of significant hyperinsulinemia with reactive hypoglycemia and liver dysfunction, whose genetic assessment was performed by using whole-exome sequencing.
