News

LATEST UPDATE: While Los Angeles County Department of Public Health has relaxed its indoor mask requirement, at this time, CSUN plans to maintain indoor mask requirements for all students, faculty and staff, regardless of vaccination status, throughout the spring semester.

Congressman Sherman Secures $1 Million for CSUN StrengthUnited to Support Children’s Mental Health Programs

StrengthUnited, a chartered center of California State University, Northridge’s Michael D. Eisner College of Education, has received $1 million from an appropriations request made by Rep. Brad Sherman (D-Sherman Oaks) included in the recently signed $1.5 trillion government spending bill. Continue reading about how Congressman Sherman Secures $1 Million for CSUN StrengthUnited.

CSUN’s 3 Wins Offering Free Saturday Morning Fitness Classes in Northeast SFV

As COVID-19 restrictions ease, people are slowly leaving their homes, reconnecting with their communities and rediscovering physical fitness. To make that transition easier, California State University, Northridge’s 3 Wins Fitness, a student-led health training and nutrition education program, is partnering with the city of San Fernando and healthcare provider Anthem, Inc. Continue reading about the Free Saturday Morning Fitness Classes.

CSUN Biology Professor MariaElena Zavala Wins Prestigious Service Award

CSUN biology professor MariaElena Zavala was awarded the prestigious Andreoli Faculty Service Award, presented to a CSU faculty member who has made outstanding contributions to the development of biotechnology programs in the CSU system. The award also recognizes a faculty member who has made significant efforts to provide access to biotechnology to underrepresented and non-traditional students. Continue reading about how Professor MariaElena Zavala Wins Prestigious Service Award.

88.5 Adds Two New Voices to Its Airwaves

88.5-FM, Southern California’s leading Triple-A (adult album alternative) rock station, is adding two new voices to its lineup — acclaimed radio personalities Julie Slater and Lesley James. Slater will be taking over the weekday midday slot, from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m., from Marc “Mookie” Kaczor, allowing him to focus on his duties as the station’s program director. Continue reading about the Two New Voices.

GradFest 2022 Returns to CSUN Campus Store

After two years of remote instruction and hybrid learning, CSUN is honored and delighted to celebrate all graduating classes from 2019-2022 at this year’s Commencement ceremonies in May. To get you ready for the big event, GradFest will be held from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. on March 8-10 at the CSUN Campus Store and will serve as a one-stop resource for graduation information. Continue reading about GradFest 2022 .
