Common Sleep Disorders

  • Insomnia

    Insomnia is a sleep disorder where you have trouble falling or staying asleep. Insomnia may be short-lived (acute), or long-lasting (chronic).

  • Sleep Apnea

    Marked by abnormal breathing at night, sleep apnea is one of the most common sleep disorders, affecting adults and children alike.

  • Narcolepsy

    Falling asleep during the day despite eight hours of sleep? Narcolepsy, a sleep disorder, may be to blame

  • Shift Work Disorder

    Many people have jobs that require them to work when they may otherwise be sleeping, resulting in poor sleep hygiene.

  • Excessive Sleepiness

    People struggling with excessive sleepiness feel sluggish and groggy throughout the day, which can lead to accidents and long-term health effects.
