Events that connect

Events and workshops for the artist in you

We host more than 100 live (in-person and virtual!) events with partners in communities all over the US every year because we believe that connection deepens our ability to both learn and relate to different perspectives.

From educational events that teach the tools of creative sustainability to screenings and live performances, we’ve got events for everyone who is simply (and powerfully) interested in being inspired to deeper connection and greater action through the arts.

Connect in the workplace

Drive employee engagement, education and intelligence with award-winning films

Seed&Spark's inclusive workplace culture solution helps companies of all sizes implement, execute and measure a regular inclusion practice — one that goes beyond meeting hiring quotas or checking HR boxes. We employ the most compelling independent short films from around the world to deprogram implicit bias, foster greater communication and sense of belonging for both employees and leaders, and inspire company-wide action.
