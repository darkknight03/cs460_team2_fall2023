Featured Partner

Save the Children has partnered with the Mars Wrigley Foundation since 2011 to elevate the importance of oral health for millions of the world’s most vulnerable girls and boys – enabling children to stay in schools and reach their full potential. Today, we are working in China, the Philippines and Vietnam to improve oral health for children in the communities we serve.
