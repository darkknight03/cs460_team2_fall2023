What is Creator™?

Creator allows the marketer to bring any design file to life by instantly transforming it into engaging, interactive, shoppable digital experiences; publishing those experiences to every consumer touchpoint, and optimizing them continuously, in real time, to maximize online sales.

The Creator Platform is the only DXP on the market that does it all in minutes, without needing to involve developers or any other resources.

1. Create it

Transform any existing design file in seconds, utilize a template, or build your digital experience from scratch. Truly create without limiting your creative vision.

2. Bring it to life

Easily add animations, interactivity, multi-merchandizing shoppability, video - anything you want - to engage customers in a unique and memorable way.

3. Launch it

Seamlessly integrate any digital experience with your entire tech stack, publish in just a few clicks, and optimize in real-time to drive more eCommerce sales.
