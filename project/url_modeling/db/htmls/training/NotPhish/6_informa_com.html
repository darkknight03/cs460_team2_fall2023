Important Notice

We are aware that members of the public have been approached by individuals claiming to be acting on behalf of Informa, or an Informa subsidiary, in connection with investments, pensions or job vacancies.

Informa never provides unsolicited investment or pensions advice and is not making offers to buy or sell any shares or recover lost investments or pension funds.

Be highly cautious of any unsolicited phone calls or correspondence about investment or recruitment matters, especially if they ask for personal or financial information or suggest the matter is urgent.

We value thinking big while keeping a constant focus on the detail and on our customers. In return, we aim to help colleagues make the most of their skills in a workplace that is open, dynamic, supportive and inclusive.

Sustainability

We're proud of our commitments to sustainable business and our achievements in embedding sustainability into everything we do.

Informa is a Carbon Neutral Company and we're accelerating our activity through the five year FasterForward programme. Our focus is on faster progress in areas where we can make the most significant positive impact: our operations, the content and knowledge we provide to customers and the communities in which we work.
