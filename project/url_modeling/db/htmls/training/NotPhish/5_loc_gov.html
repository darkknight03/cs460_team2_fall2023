Free to Use and Reuse: Diners, Drive-Ins and Restaurants

This selection of rights-free pictures highlights architecture, signage, and people at diners, drive-ins and restaurants--one in each state and the District of Columbia. All pictures are from the Prints & Photographs Division unless otherwise credited. Browse more content that is free to use and reuse

  • Restaurant cook. Mogollon, New Mexico. Photo by Russell Lee, 1940.

  • Restaurant worker Gladys Prior arranges menu board, Paterson, New Jersey. Photo by Martha Cooper, 1994. American Folklife Center.

  • Two Girls Waffle House, Anchorage, Alaska, about 1910.

  • K & J Dairy, 11600 Livernois, Detroit, Michigan. Photo by John Margolies,1986.

  • Restaurant sidewalk counter, New York City, New York. Photo by Angelo Rizzuto, 1953.

  • Nan Denton's Drive-in sign, Knoxville, Tennessee. With curb service. Photo by John Margolies, 1979.

  • Twistee Treat Ice Cream Stand, Saint Joseph, Missouri. Photo by John Margolies,1996.

  • Mel's, a classic American diner, in Grandy, North Carolina. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2017.

  • Coffee Pot Restaurant, Lexington, Virginia. Photo by John Margolies, 1982.

  • Washington Street Diner, downtown Athens, Alabama. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2010.

  • Bailey's Dairy Treat Stand, built in 1952 as Butchie's Drive-In, Hot Springs, Arkansas. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2020.

  • Chef in North Beach Italian restaurant. San Francisco, California. Photo by John Collier, 1941.

  • Customer line at the Varsity Grill, Atlanta, Georgia. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2017.

  • U.S. Naval Base, Pearl Harbor, Yard Restaurant, Seventh Street at Avenue D, Pearl City, Honolulu County, Hawaii. Photo by David Franzen, 2001.

  • Bang's Drive-In, Route 34, Chariton, Iowa. Photo by John Margolies,1980.

  • Tip Top Drive In, Lewiston, Idaho. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2005.

  • Bert's 50's Diner in rural Mechanicsville, Maryland. Photo by Carol M. Highsmith, 2017.
