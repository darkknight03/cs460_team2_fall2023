About Us

BitcoinAverage is the blockchain’s premier source of historical bitcoin price data. In 2013, we launched the world’s first Bitcoin price index, which has grown to be one of the most respected and widely cited Bitcoin Price Indices.

Starting initially with Bitcoin to USD, EUR, GBP, CAD and PLN price pairs, we have grown our dataset to now feature over 172 different fiat currencies and over 1440 cryptocurrency markets. We aggregate real-time bitcoin prices based on order books and transactions on over 50 major bitcoin exchanges around the world. This allows us to provide, through our long-established weighted average, bitcoin price data to users in accounting, finance, Bitcoin trading, Bitcoin mining, as well as to Bitcoin hobbyists.
