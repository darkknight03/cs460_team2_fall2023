Inside The USOPC

About The USOPC

Founded in 1894, the USOPC empowers Team USA athletes to achieve sustained competitive excellence and well-being.

More

Athlete Services

The USOPC supports Olympic and Paralympic athletes via a variety of programs for both athletes and their National Governing Bodies - including training facilities, sports medicine and science, coaching education, health insurance, education and career services and more.

More

Coronavirus Updates

Updates on the coronavirus pandemic from the U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Committee's medical professionals and COVID-19 working group.

Mental Health

The USOPC is committed to supporting and promoting sustained and holistic athlete well-being throughout their athletic careers.
