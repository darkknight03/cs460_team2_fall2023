EEOC CONTINUES TO SERVE THE PUBLIC DURING COVID-19 CRISIS

The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) wants you to know that we are continuing to enforce the nation's employment non-discrimination laws while ensuring that all of our activities are consistent with public health guidelines. The EEOC has closed its physical offices to the public and implemented agency-wide expanded telework. More: www.eeoc.gov/coronavirus
