    Overview

    We protect your privacy



    Protecting your privacy is a responsibility we take seriously. That includes making sure your data is safe and secure, and that you have control.
    To provide a more personalized shopping experience, we do share some information with third parties, who may have a secondary use of this information.
    Here, you can learn about how we use cookies and choose whether or not to allow advertising cookies in your online experience with Ulta.

    Privacy Policy

    • Required Cookies

      Required Cookies

      Always Active

      About Required Cookies

      These cookies do not store any personally-identifiable information and are necessary for our website to function. Required cookies are used when saving your settings and preferences, when you log in and out of your account, and for other basic site functions. These cookies cannot be disabled.

    • Advertising Cookies

      Advertising Cookies

      About advertising cookies

      Advertising cookies are used to provide you with a personalized experience. These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests. These cookies do not directly store your personal information, but they do support the ability to uniquely identify your internet browser and device. If you do not allow these cookies to be used, either by your browser settings or if you select "No (Opt-Out)" in the toggle above, you will experience less targeted advertising from our partners.

      Please note: Clearing your browser cookies at any time will undo preferences saved here. To re-save preferences, please return to this screen after clearing your cookies. Also, please note that the option selected here will only apply to the device you are currently using. You may also opt out of advertising cookies from any other devices you use to access our website or mobile applications.

    • Performance Cookies

      Performance Cookies

      About Performance Cookies

      These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.

      All information these cookies collect is aggregated and, therefore, anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor its performance.
