The payment is made in monthly payments with a fixed term until you fully repay the money you owe. As compared to credit cards, installment loans have a fixed end date. So, if you are a good payer and settle the amount loaned in time, you will have a good credit rating and will be able to lend a higher amount in the future.

Installment loans are either secured (collateral) or unsecured (without guarantee). For instance, if you apply for a mortgage loan, your collateral is the property you are buying. If you are not able to pay your mortgage loan, the lending company will take over the property. It is an example of a secured or with a collateral loan.

The unsecured loan or without collateral, on the other hand, depends on how high and low your credit score is. This type of loan has a higher interest rate. Payday Installment Loans – The Process of Getting One

Payday installment loans are yet another type of loan that people get to help them with their immediate needs. To help you with the process below is a short guide in getting payday installment loans online.

Step One: Look for the right lenders

If this is your first time to get payday installment loans, you might be confused as to which lending company to trust. Start your search using Google and type on the search tab installment loans near me.

Most of the borrowers these days prefer online lenders to solve their money problems. It’s because almost all the transactions are done online, and it is much faster and secure. Also, checking the lending companies online gives you a clear idea of the financial market and the services being offered by each company.

Step Two: Fundamental Requirements

To get approved for a payday loan, a borrower should meet some requirements. If it is installment loans no credit check, then the company will not perform any credit background check. They will request your proof of identity, income, and employment. So make sure all the vital information needed is at hand.

Step Three: Filing the Application

You can fill-up the application form either online or in the office. With the technology these days, lenders accept applications via email. However, some lenders still prefer to have them filled-up in the office.

One of the requirements of many lenders is for the borrower to have a checking account. If not, the application will be disapproved. However, some lending firms accept a savings account.

Step Four: Signing of Agreement

If the application is made online, it will also be signed online. Some sign the contract in person. Based on the present legislation, the lenders should disclose the terms & conditions of repayment to a borrower before the contract is signed.

Step Five: Wait for Approval

For payday installment loans, the approval will not take that long. Most of the time, the applicant is contacted within a few hours.

Step Six: Getting the Money

Once you get approved, the money is then sent to you electronically to the applicant’s bank account. The process takes only less than 24 hours.
