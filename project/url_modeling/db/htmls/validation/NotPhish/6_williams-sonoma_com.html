Pascualina!! That’s today’s lunch menu! Pascualina is a fancy name for the spinach pie many Argentines typically make & eat at home. It was one of the few ways my grandma or mom could make any of us eat veggies! Yet it is simple to make and super delicious! A simple pie crust is filled with a scrumptious cheese and spinach filling that includes Parmesan, ricotta, mozzarella and sautéed spinach and onions. I used a ceramic pie funnel shaped like Hedwig (Harry’s owl!) created by lecreuset and available through williamssonoma to channel the steam out of the pie while in the oven. So fun! But if you’re don’t have a pie funnel you can simply make some small incisions in the center of the pie with a knife. The recipe will be on the blog by the weekend! How is your Thursday going?
