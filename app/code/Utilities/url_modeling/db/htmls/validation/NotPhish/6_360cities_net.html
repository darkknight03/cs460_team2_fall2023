Stansted is a Grade II Listed Edwardian country house set on a 1,750-acre estate within the South Downs National Park. Situated on the Hampshire/Sussex border between Chichester and Portsmouth, the house began as a hunting lodge in the 11th century. Developed throughout the centuries, the current house was built in 1902.
