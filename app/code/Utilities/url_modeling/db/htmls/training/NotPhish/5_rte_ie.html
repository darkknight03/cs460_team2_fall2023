Protecting your privacy

RTÉ.ie uses cookies to allow us to better understand our users. The stored information will not normally identify you directly. You can choose not to allow some types of cookies below but please be aware that this may impact your experience of the site and the services that we can offer.
For more information read our Cookie Policy and our Privacy Policy.

Manage Consent Preferences

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are essential so that you can move around the site and use its features. They cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to certain requests, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then function. These cookies do not store any information that can identify you personally.

Functional Cookies

These cookies help us understand our audience better so we can keep on giving you what you love. By counting visits and traffic sources, these cookies help us to measure and improve our performance and content. They let us know which pages are the most and least popular and show us how you’re moving around the site. All information in these cookies is aggregated and anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies we will not know when you have visited our site, and we will not be able to monitor our performance.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us remember your choices, so you don’t have to keep giving us the same information again and again. They also make enhanced personalised features and an improved experience possible. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, then some or all of these services may not function properly.

Targeting Cookies and Third-Party Embeds

This category of cookies is used for advertising and certain embedded social media content and may be set by our advertising or social partners. These cookies may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device.
