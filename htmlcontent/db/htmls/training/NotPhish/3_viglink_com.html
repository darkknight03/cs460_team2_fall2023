Automated Link Monetization

Automate your affiliate marketing with our //Commerce JavaScript. It works in the background to transform your existing product links into monetized affiliate links. Connect to over 60,000 merchants, seamlessly linking content, audience, and product. Whether it’s from a commission or a click, our technology makes sure you’re always earning as much as possible with built-in optimization. A single snippet of code is all it takes to create a brand new source of revenue.

Works with Google AMP.

Replay

Affiliate Links for Social Networks

Affiliate marketing works for creators of all kinds. //Commerce lets you post affiliate links on your favorite social networks, in your emails, or anywhere you don’t want to use our JavaScript. It’s the perfect solution for influencers, editors, and marketers.

Create Links from Your Browser

Simplify your editorial workflow and find great products faster with our Chrome extension. Just navigate to any product page, and instantly create an affiliate link. Plus, you’ll see other retailers that sell the same product, which will pay you the most, and all the available coupons for that product. Then, just share the link anywhere you like: websites, social networks, and even emails. It’s the easiest, fastest way to create affiliate links.

Make strategic decisions using our performance metrics.

Track all of the metrics critical to your e-commerce strategy in one place, using our Publisher Dashboard. Gain insight into audience trends, learn what links are earning the most, and determine which merchants deliver the highest earnings per click.

Easy access to top brands & merchants
