Oil volatility - the next big short?

In case you have missed it, oil volatility, OVX, has been moving in tandem with oil. The up panic has been much stronger than the down panic, but are people getting ahead of themselves when it comes to pricing oil volatility? Consensus is strong about oil going to the moon, but what if oil needs a long consolidation as the next narrative? War news is old news. Second chart shows the huge gap between OVX and VIX. Is it impossible for oil to take a pause and become slightly more boring. If that happens, oil vol is very rich up here.
