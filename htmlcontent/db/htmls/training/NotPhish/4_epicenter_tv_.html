Since 2014, Epicenter is educating the crypto community

Every week, we interview business leaders, engineers academics and entrepreneurs, and bring you a diverse spectrum of opinions and points of view. Tens of thousands of people tune in each week to learn and gain a deep understanding of issues facing the blockchain industry. Since 214, episodes have been downloaded over 5 million times.   Epicenter is hosted by Sebastien Couture, Brian Fabian Crain, Meher Roy, Sunny Aggarwal and Friederieke Ernst.
