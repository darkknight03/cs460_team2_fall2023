Description

Install Lisk Wallet for safe and easy transactions in a secure crypto wallet. Monitor the price and exchange LSK directly in the app. Your coins will be protected even if you lose your phone or forget your private keys, thanks to high-level security features.


Advanced security

Lisk Wallet powered by Freewallet is a secure blockchain wallet for your cryptocurrency. We will protect your digital cash even if you lose your phone. Lock your account and retrieve access to your wallet from another device.


Security features include:

● Fingerprint Login.

● 2-factor authentication.

● PIN code lock.

● Email confirmation for each transaction.

● Daily and weekly transaction limits.


What’s more, we keep a majority of our coins in secure offline storage. It increases safety hackers won’t have access to your funds. We can guarantee that no money will ever be lost or stolen from storage.


Exchange functions

Deposit almost any cryptocurrency in Lisk Wallet and it will be automatically exchanged to LSK. Make secure transactions to almost any altcoin address. All transactions between Freewallet customers are completely free. No need to visit exchanges, as we’ve got a crypto converter right in the application. Monitor the balance of Lisk in fiat currencies, check real-time price charts and exchange LSK directly in the wallet.


Easy and comfortable to use

Enjoy a user-friendly and beautiful interface while using Lisk Wallet. Log in with your email, Facebook, Google+ or mobile phone number and share your wallet address on Twitter, Facebook, email, etc.


Caring Support

Our support team is ready to help you however you need it, 24/7. Lisk Wallet supports 13 languages including French, German, Chinese, Spanish, and Russian.


About Freewallet

Freewallet has been a key player of the cryptocurrency community since 2016. It supports more than 50+ cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dogecoin, Monero, and Bytecoin.


About Lisk

Lisk is a digital currency based on sidechain technology. It allows developers to create autonomous blockchains linked to Lisk’s main chain. Lisk has JavaScript support, which simplifies the development process of dApps (decentralized applications).
