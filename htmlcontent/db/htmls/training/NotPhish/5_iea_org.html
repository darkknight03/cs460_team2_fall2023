Communiqué

The Year of Implementation: Accelerating Global Action on Clean Energy and Energy Security

Energy and climate ministers from IEA Member, Association and Accession countries, and other key partners, met in Paris on 23 and 24 March for the 2022 IEA Ministerial Meeting, which comes at a critical time for the global economy and energy markets following Russia’s invasion of Ukraine .The International Energy Agency (IEA) Governing Board, at ministerial level, has adopted the 2022 IEA ministerial communiqué.

2022 IEA Ministerial Communiqué
Statement

IEA Member Statement

Today International Energy Agency Ministers convened for a discussion on global energy security and clean energy deployment at the 2022 IEA Ministerial Meeting. They condemned, in the strongest possible terms, Russia’s unlawful invasion of Ukraine
