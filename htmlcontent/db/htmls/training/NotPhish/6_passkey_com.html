Room block management with Passkey®

No matter the size of next event, Passkey makes it easy to manage your room blocks and hotel bookings online. 

Request a Passkey demo

*All fields are required.

Registration is just the beginning

Your attendees are registered for your event, but they still need a place to sleep. Passkey bridges the gap between registration and your room block, so you can sleep easy knowing that your attendees will, too.

Passkey makes it easy for your attendees to book a room during registration. So you'll fill your contracted room blocks faster and reduce attrition risk.

Say goodbye to rooming list spreadsheets! Bookings made through Passkey feed directly into hotels' reservation system.

With real-time reporting on pickup rates, available rooms, and more, Passkey gives you valuable insights on the status of your room blocks.

Automate your attendees' hotel bookings, because you've got more important things to do with your time.

Filling your room block just got simpler.

Seamless registration

Deliver a seamless registration and hotel booking process with Event Connector, a proprietary API, linking your Cvent event registration to Passkey’s hotel booking website.

Smartalerts™

Get automated alerts at critical event milestones, such as weekly pick-up reports or when your room block reaches a pre-defined threshold. Alerts ensure your planning staff receives the right information at the right time to make accurate inventory, pricing and allocation decisions.

Event tracking

Track real-time room block productivity, run reports and manage rooming lists all within an event dashboard. Additionally, sub-block contacts also have the ability to manage their allocated inventory through their own dashboard.

Web and mobile booking

Manage room blocks, sub-blocks, room lists and reservations from anywhere, on any device. Attendees also have the benefit of booking hotel rooms online or from any mobile device and can purchase room upgrade options, extended stays and more.
