Description

Mobi is a multi-currency digital assets management tool that goes beyond wallet storage. All you need is to register with your phone number to send, store, and transfer Bitcoin and at least 18 other popular cryptocurrencies. Mobi also provides financial services such as Bitcoin conversion to multiple fiat currencies, OTC (over-the-counter) trade, and a savings return function.


What you can do with Mobi


Send, receive, and store Bitcoin (BTC) and at least 18 other popular crypto currencies, including Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Litecoin (LTC), Ether (ETH), Ripple (XRP), Dash (DASH), Bitcoin Gold (BTG), Tether (USDT) , Zcash (ZEC) and more within your secure wallet.


Convert your Bitcoin to multiple fiat currencies worldwide to lock-in the exchange rate especially when you believe the value of cryptocurrencies will drop.


Transfer funds instantly to your friends and family within Mobi for free or to any phone number worldwide.

Cross-border payments made easy for Mobi users! Say goodbye to those 5-8% banking fees.


Mobi is a one-stop digital wallet that allows you to manage 20 popular cryptocurrencies:

Easy to Use, Supports Litecoin and Bitcoin Cash, and Conversion of BTC To 150 Fiat Currencies!

I think this is probably the easiest bitcoin wallet that I have tried out. The interface is pretty useful and intuitive, and I can send and receive quickly.


When I did deeper, though, I found that there are some features that are pretty cool like converting bitcoin to about 150(?) different fiat currencies. (I counted.) AND you can convert between the currencies themselves. So it effectively acts as a forex app or a bitcoin exchange where you can hedge/speculate about price movements. Pretty nifty.


One other thing is that this is probably one of a few litecoin and bitcoin cash wallets. So that’s also cool. Overall pretty satisfied. I’ll try to update this review as I use the app more.

Jonlevi , 12/03/2017

Pretty interface, highly irresponsible security setup

While this app does have a pretty and easy to use interface it is inevitably going to result in many people losing their funds.

Not only does the app not give a real big coin wallet with an actual private seed back up, they failed to give the user any possible means of backing up the wall it securely. This means that when you lose your phone, your bit coin is irrevocably gone. The makers of this wallet that have sacrificed the core principles of what bitcoin is intended to be for

the sake of convenience and flashiness. It’s hard to be around bank when you’re given no possibility of protecting yourself, and absolutely zero explanation of how to do so.


As if this weren’t enough, requiring you to give your phone number and name, and using that information as a highly insecure form of two factor authentication makes this wallet an absolute no go. Get CoPay instead.
