    In the Climate Action Blog
    What is ACE?

    Greenhouse gas emissions are caused by the action, inaction, decisions and behaviour of people, which is why educating, empowering and engaging people to tackle climate change is vital. Action for Climate Empowerment (ACE) is the foundation for a low-emission, climate-resilient and just future, thanks to its six interconnected elements: climate change education, training, public awareness, public participation, public access to information, and international cooperation on these elements.
