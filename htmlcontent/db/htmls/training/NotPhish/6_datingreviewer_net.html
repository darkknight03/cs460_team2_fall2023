FAQ About Online Dating

How many people use dating apps?

During the COVID period, more and more people start using dating sites. According to the statistics, more than 30% of US adult men/women have used a dating app or site intending to find friends, hookups, or soulmates.

Which dating sites are completely free?

Below you’ll find the listing of the best dating apps without commission to pay: Tinder, Bumble, Match, OKCupid, Plenty of Fish, Hinge, and Facebook Dating. You can join all of them free of charge. Some of them offer extra features for premium members, but they are not obligatory.

What dating app should I use?

If you are new to the online dating world, start using Match or Tinder. You will find all types of people and relationships there. Otherwise, you can use niche-based sites/apps like SilverSingles or ChristianMingle.

Is online dating safe?

Online dating sites and apps are a very efficient and practical way to date new people in your area or foreign countries. According to the statistics, approximately 60 percent of US people have had positive online dating experiences with popular platforms.

How to make sure that the dating app or site is safe?

You should avoid connecting with suspicious men/women, check out your future date on other social media platforms, block/report scammers immediately, do not rush to share your details, never give financial help, and video chat before dating in person.

Why dating sites don’t work?

Many happy couples can prove the opposite. All the mentioned sites in this post give excellent results within a short time period. You just need to understand your needs and deal with safe and efficient online dating platforms.

Is it possible to find serious relationships online?

Note that not all dating sites cater to singles with serious intentions. You must be sure that you use the right site. If you want to build a family, avoid sites/apps that offer easy hookups only. Match, Tinder, eHarmony are the best platforms to look for long-lasting relationships.
