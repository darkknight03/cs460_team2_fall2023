Root Zone Update Process Study
The objectives of the Root Zone Update Process Study were to investigate whether there is a need to increase (and if so, how) the robustness of the operational arrangements for making changes to the root zone content, identify any single points of failure that may exist and, should they exist, offer recommendations on how to reduce or eliminate them.
Closes: 25 April 2022
