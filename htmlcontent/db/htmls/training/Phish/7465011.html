The Artist

Ryan Robinson is a digital native artist who has been working for the past decade on artistic projects for major hip hop artists like Kodak Black, Juice WRLD, XXXTentacion, 21 Savage, Gucci Mane or Playboi Carti. In 2022, the artist known as Rhabbitz on social media, decided to launch his very first NFT collection. The reason? “NFTs are probably one of the only platform where artists can connect with a community and be represented and appreciated for their creativity.”

THE CREATIVE PROCESS :

“I like the balance of cute with semi realistic aesthetics: make it bright but also have a sense of realism that’s why all the bunnies are half black.That’s how my life is. I’m a guy that has a bright side and a dark side like everyone and I like to use both in my creative process” - Ryan Robinson (Yobritish)

Roadmap

Our roadmap 1.0 will be updated according to the project's evolution.

A PIECE OF POP CULTURE

After collaborating with major Hip Hop and Rap celebrities, Rhabbitz is joining forces with entrepreneurs, investors, influencers and tech geniuses to introduce his best artwork to the NFT Space. The collection of 8888 NFTs possess over 150 traits coming straight from the artist’s creative mind. Get yourself a buddy, mint a bunny in February 2022.

BB MERCHANDISING

Real buddies always have you back, so you can count on the bunnies to add a little twist to your wardrobe with some exclusive merchandising. The items will be designed by the artist himself.

THE LEGENDARY BUNNIES

Exclusive 1/1 Bunny Buddies will be created by the artist to be at the center of his collection. These 15 unique items will be released after the big reveal. This process will encourage new investors to join the project, build the hype and stimulate the volume traded. Any future holder has a chance to mint randomly a Legendary Bunny.

CHARITY

As this collection dives into the duality in every human, the artist has decided to make a donation of $50.000 to a mental health charity for children and young adults.

NEW BUDDIES IN THE METAVERSE

The Bunny Buddies will make their grand entrance in the Metaverse through special events in collaboration with brands and other successful projects before acquiring lands and building an entire ecosystem in the new world.

EVOLUTION OF THE BUNNIES

The artist will create super carrots that will be airdropped to some of our holders. Your bunny might want to take a bite out of this delicious snack but be careful… This might trigger some irremediable changes in your bunny’s personality.

THE MORE THE MERRIER

This is only the beginning of Rhabbitz’s creative process. More buddies will join the Bunnies in their crazy adventures. Holders and early members of the community will have the priority on future drops.
