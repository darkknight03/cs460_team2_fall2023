Cybercrime Marketplace Owner Added to FBI’s Cyber Most Wanted List

The US Department of Justice (DoJ) recently indicted a Russian national for allegedly creating and maintaining a cybercrime marketplace. The suspect was also placed on the FBI’s Cyber Most Wanted list on charges of Access Device Fraud, Wire Fraud and Aggravated Identity Theft. Igor Dekhtyarchuk was indicted in Texas for managing a cybercrime market that hosted a broad range of illicit products, including stolen credit cards, personal information and unauthorized access to compromised devices.
